About Anton Ego

Milestones

January 12, 1951: Born to Anton and Agathe Ego in Paris, France.

January 13, 1951: Went to my first restaurant in Paris, France. I spit out the food, so you could say it was my first review.

May 12, 1953: I said my first word: perspective. I waited to say any other word until I could say this one.

September 25, 1956: I tried ratatouille for the first time.

October 4, 1957: I tried ratatouille for the second time.

November 12, 1958: I tried ratatouille for the third time.

December 24, 1959: I tried ratatouille for the fourth time.

August 3, 1962: I tried ratatouille for the fifth time.

August 4, 1962: I tried ratatouille for the sixth time.

September 1, 1974: Began my schooling at University of Paris to study Food Criticism.

June 29, 2007: Starred in the critically-acclaimed film Ratatouille.
